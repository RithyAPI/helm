1. Access Grafana:
   - Port-forward:
       kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "grafana.fullname" . }} 3000:{{ .Values.service.port }}
       http://localhost:3000

2. Login:
   - Username/Password from Secret (GF_SECURITY_ADMIN_USER / GF_SECURITY_ADMIN_PASSWORD).

3. Prometheus datasource:
   - Pre-provisioned from values.datasource.prometheus.url
